Adulting 101

About

Adulting 101 is a website designed for first generation college students to mitigate the problems that come with transitioning to college. This website encompasses tools and resources that help with common problems such as housing, professional development, social/academic adjustment, savings and more.

Finances


First generation students have higher drop-out rates and are more likely to take more than four years to graduate. These students need access to financial resources in order to manage their money throughout their college career.
